About Me:

Hi! I’m Bryan, a senior at Dartmouth College double majoring in Computer Science modified with Digital Arts and Environmental Studies. I specialize in developing innovative solutions that blend technology and creativity, with experience in 3D modeling, interactive digital media, and XR design.

Throughout my academic career, I’ve built projects like HangSmart, an intelligent outfit-planning system, and Crisis Companion, a data visualization tool for enhancing communication during emergencies. These experiences have deepened my passion for using technology to tell stories and solve complex problems.

As I prepare for graduate studies in Computer Science with a focus on Digital Arts, I’m excited to continue exploring how emerging technologies can shape the future of interactive experiences.

Outside of work, I enjoy photography, virtual reality gaming, botany , and advocating for environmental sustainability through creative media.

Feel free to explore my projects and connect with me!
