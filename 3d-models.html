This project involved designing and rendering a 3D room using Maya. The objective was to create a visually engaging environment by focusing on detailed modeling, realistic lighting, and textures.

To create a realistic 3D room that demonstrates proficiency in modeling, texturing, and lighting techniques while conveying a cohesive and visually appealing aesthetic.

Goal:
Texturing:

Applied high-resolution textures for surfaces like wood flooring, bedding, and the fish tank.
Experimented with different materials to create reflective and matte finishes.

Lighting:

Implemented a combination of ambient lighting and targeted light sources (e.g., bedside lamps, computer monitor glow).
Adjusted intensity and color to create a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Modeling:

Modeled furniture, decor, and room structures from scratch, focusing on proportions and functionality.
Included intricate details such as books, lamps, and a computer monitor to enhance realism.

Key Features:

A functional, lived-in room design.
Use of lighting and textures to create mood and realism.
Attention to detail in both small and large-scale elements.
